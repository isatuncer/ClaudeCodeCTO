## 3. Cookie Preferences
- A cookie banner appears on first visit
- Default: only essential cookies are active
- You can give consent by category
- You can change your preferences at any time: [Cookie Settings link]

## 4. How Do I Delete Cookies?
You can delete cookies from your browser settings:
- Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
- Firefox: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
- Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies

**Note:** Deleting essential cookies may cause the service to not function properly.
